What happens next

As a self-funder of care services, you are free to make any arrangements you choose and pay the providers directly.

If you would like to get some assistance in finding suitable service providers, please contact the Adult Services Access Team on 01724 297000.

When your savings and investments reduce to less than £23,250 you will qualify for financial support. To apply for financial support you will need to complete a Financial Assessment form. We recommend you apply slightly before your capital falls below £23,250 to allow time for your application to be processed.
