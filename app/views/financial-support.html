Eligible for financial support

You will have to pay a contribution to the cost of your care, but only from your income

How do we work out how much your contribution will be?

We follow a simple 4 step process.

Example

Mr. Jenkins is 72 and lives on his own. He receives a home support service arranged through the council. The cost of his service is £55.00 per week

  1. Step 1 Add together all of your different income

    List the different sources of income. Here is an example:

    1. Income

    2. £125.00 per week - State Retirement Pension

    3. £45.00 per week - British Steel Pension

    4. £85.60 per week - Attendance Allowance (High Rate)

    5. £57.30 per week - Pension Credit (Guarantee Credit)

    6. £13.40 per week - Pension Credit (Savings Credit)

    7. £326.30 per week - Total Weekly Income

  2. Step 2 Add up any income we don't count

    There are some things we exclude, for example:

    1. Discounts and allowances

    2. £13.40 per week - Pension Credit (Savings Credit)

    3. £28.30 per week - Attendance Allowance

    4. £7.05 per week - Disability Related Expenditure

    5. £203.75 per week - Income Support +25%

    6. £252.50 per week - Total Weekly Discounts and allowances

  3. Step 3 Calculate total income that can be used for contributions

    Take the total weekly discounts and allowances from the total weekly income.

    1. £326.30 per week - Total Weekly Income

    2. -£252.50 per week - Total Weekly Discounts and allowances

    3. £73.80 per week - Weekly contribution

  4. Step 4 Compare the assessed income with the total cost of care

    We then compare the total assessed income (£73.80), with the total cost of services (£55.00). Mr. Jenkins would pay whichever amount is lower.

    He will pay £55.00 per week, but if the cost of Mr. Jenkins services was to increase in the future, he would pay no more than £73.80 per week in contributions.

What happens next

If you are having, or have already had a Care Assessment and want to know exactly how much you will pay for services or you want to apply for financial support, you can use the BetterCare tool to complete a Financial Assessment.
