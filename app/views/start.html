Before you start

The amount of money you have affects how much financial assistance we might give you. When we ask how much capital you have we mean the total value of your savings and investments, so you will need to have a good idea of the value of your savings and investments. This includes:

Savings
  • Bank current accounts
  • Building society accounts
  • Deposit accounts or special investment accounts. This includes money held in the National Savings Bank, Girobank, and Trustee Savings Bank
  • ISAs
  • SAYE schemes
  • Unit trusts
  • Post Office Card Accounts (Watch this video for guidance on adding information about Post Office Card Accounts)
  • Co-operatives share accounts
  • Cash
Investments
  • National Savings Certificates and Ulster Savings Certificates
  • Premium Bonds
  • Stocks and Shares
  • Capital held by the Court of Protection or a Deputy appointed by that Court
  • Trust Funds
